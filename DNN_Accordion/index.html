New student (first time registering)

New students

As a student registering with the Disability Services for the first time, you will need to: 

  1. Fill out the Accommodation Request Form
    1. Need help? Check out our video:

      How to Submit an Accommodation Request Form
  2. Submit Documentation of the Disability
    1. Documentation of the Disability must be submitted in order to move forward with the accommodation process. Without documentation, it may cause a delay in the student receiving needed accommodations. For a description of accepted forms of Disability Documentation click here.
    2. Documentation of Disability can be submitted using the following methods:
      1. Accommodation Request Form
      2. Student's Accommodate account
        Need some help? Check out our video

        How to Access Accommodate
  3. Register for Courses
    1. It is necessary for the student to sign up for courses preferably before making an appointment with a Disability Specialist.
  4. Make an Appointment with the Disability Services Office
    1. It is necessary for the student to meet with the Disability Specialist, preferably after the student has signed up for courses. It is recommended to do so approximately two weeks before classes begin or within the first two weeks of class. Accommodations are not retroactive so it helps to have them in place sooner rather than later.
    2. At this meeting, the Disability Specialist will interview the student and together they will make a plan for accommodating the student's disability.
    3. A student can make an appointment with our office using their Accommodate account.  Need some help? Check out our videos:

How to Access Accommodate

How to Make an Appointment

For end of the semester requests:
  • Remember to plan ahead. Our office is very busy at the end of the semester for accommodation requests.
  • We recommended that steps 1, 2, and 4 as listed above, to be completed at least three to four weeks before the semester is over to ensure that our office can process your accommodation request in time for your finals. 
  • Waiting until a week before or the week of finals to register with our office presents many challenges to process and approve your request in time for your finals.

New Students(first time registering)

New students

Please note Housing Accommodations are given based on availability. Filling out the Housing Accommodations form will not guarantee you Housing Accommodations.

If you have requested academic accommodations/housing accomodations with the Disability Services Office you already have an Accommodate account and should refer to returning student instructions.

Read the following instructions carefully before submitting:

This is your first time making a request with the Disability Services Office for an accommodation. To begin the process please complete each of the following steps:

  1. Fill out the Accommodation Request Form
    1. Need help? Check out our video:

      How to Submit an Accommodation Request Form
  2. Submit Documentation of the Disability
    1. Documentation of the Disability must be submitted in order to move forward with the accommodation process. Without documentation, it may cause a delay in the student receiving needed accommodations. For a description of accepted forms of Disability Documentation click here.
    2. Documentation of Disability can be submitted using the following methods:
      1. Accommodation Request Form
      2. Student's Accommodate account
        Need some help? Check out our video

        How to Access Accommodate
  3. Make an Appointment with the Disability Services Office
    1. It is necessary for the student to meet with the Disability Specialist.
    2. At this meeting, the Disability Specialist will interview the student and together they will make a plan for accommodating the student's disability.
    3. A student can make an appointment with our office using their Accommodate account. Need some help? Check out our videos

Returning Students (previously registered)

Returning Students

Please note Housing Accommodations are given based on availability. Filling out the Housing Accommodations form will not guarantee you Housing Accommodations.

To avoid errors, read the following each of the following prompts carefully before submitting the appropriate form for housing accommodations:

  • You were previously approved for housing accommodations through the Disability Services Office and would like to renew your request for the upcoming semester, submit a Semester request form (left navigation menu). You will only be able to do this if a housing accommodation you are looking to renew was previously approved.
    • Check out these videos for guidance: 
      • How to Access Accommodate
      • How to Submit a Semester Request Form for Housing Accommodations
  • You currently have or had academic accommodations with the Disability Services Office but this is your first time requesting housing accommodations, please submit the supplemental semester request form (left navigation menu). You will only be able to do this if you have a history of requesting academic accommodations with our office and were previously approved.
    • Check out this video for guidance: 
      • How to Access Accommodate
      • How to Submit a Semester Request Form for Housing Accommodations

If none of the above apply, this may be your first time making an accommodation request with the Disability Services Office. Please refer to the New Student Section for instructions.

  • If you receive an error that means you already have a record with us and will need to refer to one of the two prompts above to proceed

For any questions, please email us: DSO@calarts.edu
